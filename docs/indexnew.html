Who We Are

YES (Yielded Evangelical Servants) is a U.S.-based, missionary-founded nonprofit dedicated to advancing the Gospel among the nations. For over 35 years, we have served missionaries, churches, and ministry partners through accountability, field care, training, global mobilization, and donor support — with a field-focused administrative model that maximizes impact and minimizes overhead.

Why We Exist

The Need

Many believers feel called to missions but lack support, training, accountability, or a sending structure. Churches want to send missionaries, but don’t always have the systems to do it.

Our Response

YES exists to bridge that gap — so every calling can become reality, and the Gospel reaches where it has not yet been heard.
