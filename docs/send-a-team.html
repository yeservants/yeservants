Send a Team. Make an Impact.

Whether you are a church, school, or university, YES can help you organize a meaningful mission trip that serves others and strengthens your group’s faith.

Our short-term mission experiences are designed to build lasting relationships, support local ministries, and open the hearts of those who go.

We work with trusted partners in several regions of the world, including Costa Rica, Colombia, the Philippines, Kenya, and beyond, offering safe, well-structured trips for churches, youth groups, and Christian schools.

Interested in sending a team? Send us an email at info@yeservants.org including your phone number and a brief description of your idea (destination, dates, type of trip, number of people, etc.).

Our team will contact you as soon as possible to talk through the possibilities and help you start planning. We look forward to serving with you!
